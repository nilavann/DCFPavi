Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of logistics services to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our transportation services include part truck load, full truck load, over dimensional cargo, and closed vehicle options, based on your specific requirements.

You can request a quote by filling out the form on our website or by contacting our customer support team directly. We will work with you to understand your specific logistics needs and provide you with a customized quote.

Our business hours are from 9am to 5pm, Monday through Friday. However, we also offer 24/7 support for urgent logistics needs.

We take the safety and security of your shipments very seriously. We use state-of-the-art tracking and monitoring systems to ensure that your shipments are safe and secure at all times. Additionally, we work with trusted partners and carriers who share our commitment to safety and security.

In the unlikely event that your shipment is delayed or lost, we will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly and efficiently as possible. We offer full tracking and monitoring of all shipments, and we have a comprehensive insurance policy to cover any losses or damages.
